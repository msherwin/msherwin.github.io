About Me

I am a Computer Science major at the University of Scranton, entering my final semester. I started out my college career as a Chemical Engineer (yuck) at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, where I met a lot of great people and realized that I hate engineering. I was initially interested in nanotechnology, but I learned that interest in the products of a discipline is not the same thing as interest in the practice of it. I always knew I wanted to do something involving coding, but I thought I could bull my way through studies that I hated and do it on the side. I was wrong about that, but now I'm doing what I like, so I think it was worth it.

My hobbies are drawing, playing piano, and writing. I'm not very good at any of them, but I enjoy it so that's all that matters!

I also play video games. I'm actually pretty good at that. I don't play many competitive games, but I will have Smash Bros. throwdown any day of the week.
